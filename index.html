The Crossroads of Europe and Asia

Discover the landscapes, traditions, and history of Georgia.

Geography

Georgia is located at the intersection of Eastern Europe and Western Asia, bordered by the Black Sea, Russia, Turkey, Armenia, and Azerbaijan.

Culture

Georgian culture is known for its hospitality, folk music, unique alphabet, and one of the oldest wine-making traditions in the world.

History

Georgia has a recorded history spanning more than 3,000 years and has maintained a strong national identity despite periods of foreign rule.
